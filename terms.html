📜 Terms & Conditions

By enrolling in any program or booking an appointment at Ayushman PhysioFIT Rehabilitation Centre, you agree to the following terms and conditions.

1. Online Fitness Class Program

  • Classes are conducted 3 times per week — Monday, Wednesday & Friday.
  • Each membership is valid for 1 calendar month from the date of enrollment.
  • Monthly fee is ₹2,400 (inclusive of all taxes).
  • Premium video access is granted within 24 hours of payment confirmation.
  • Videos are accessible only during the enrolled time slot.

2. Appointments

  • Appointment slots are available from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM.
  • Patients must arrive 10 minutes before their scheduled time.
  • Late arrivals may result in a shortened session without refund.
  • Appointments can be rescheduled up to 24 hours in advance.

3. Patient Responsibilities

  • Patients must disclose all medical conditions before starting any program.
  • Follow all instructions given by the physiotherapist during sessions.
  • Inform the clinic immediately if you experience any discomfort or adverse effects.
  • Maintain respectful conduct during all online and offline sessions.

4. Clinic Rights

  • Ayushman PhysioFIT reserves the right to reschedule or cancel sessions due to unforeseen circumstances.
  • The clinic may terminate membership without refund in case of misconduct.
  • Session content, videos, and materials are the intellectual property of Ayushman PhysioFIT.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Ayushman PhysioFIT collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, phone number, age, and weight during enrollment or appointment booking.
  • Medical condition and treatment history for physiotherapy purposes.
  • Payment information (processed securely via Razorpay — we do not store card details).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and manage your appointments and fitness classes.
  • To send appointment confirmations and reminders via WhatsApp.
  • To provide personalized physiotherapy and fitness recommendations.
  • To process payments and issue receipts.

3. Data Security

  • All data is stored securely on Google Firebase with industry-standard encryption.
  • Payment transactions are secured by Razorpay with 256-bit SSL encryption.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Your Rights

  • You may request deletion of your personal data at any time.
  • You may opt out of WhatsApp communications by informing us.
  • Contact us at 6302478412 for any data-related requests.
🔐 We are committed to protecting your health data and personal information in compliance with applicable data protection laws.
